## Common questions

### Where are my saves?

On the device you played them on. Optional iCloud sync (Settings → iCloud) keeps your career-best, trophies, and nation name across your own devices.

### My reign reset itself / a card I expected didn't appear.

The deck deliberately randomises across reigns to keep variety high — there are 20,000+ cards across 89 thematic decks, and you'll only ever see a subset on each playthrough.

If you think a card was unfairly cut, a flag chain broke, or a feature regressed, email me with as much detail as you can. The more specific, the easier to fix.

### Can I export my chronicle?

Yes. Reach the chronicle from the game-over screen or from a previously-finished reign → tap the **download** icon at the top. The app saves a markdown file (and the newspaper-poster image) to your chosen destination via the iOS share sheet.

### Where do my Weekly Safety Backups live?

Files app → Browse → iCloud Drive → **Presidenter** folder.

One JSON file per local week, named with the date. Useful if you ever need to manually restore on a new phone or want a long-term record.

### How do I turn off Game Center / iCloud sync?

- **Game Center:** Settings (iOS) → Game Center → toggle off.
- **iCloud Key-Value sync:** Settings (iOS) → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud → Presidenter → toggle off.
- **Weekly Safety Backup:** in the app, Settings → iCloud → Weekly Safety Backup → toggle off.

### How do I reset my progress?

In the app: Settings → Data → **Reset Progress**. The alert spells out exactly what's erased. Settings (sound, haptics, motion, contrast) and your country / leader name are preserved.

### How do I replay the tutorial?

Settings → Help → **Replay Tutorial**. The 3-card intro will run again on your next term.

### I keep getting Election Day / Throne Rite. What is this?

Every 100 days, your reign faces a structural test. If you picked **Mandate** (the default reign style), the public votes; you need an approval rating of 50+ to continue. If you picked **Throne**, the cabinet weighs your loyalty instead.

Pick your style on the New Reign setup screen.

### I have an idea for a card / feature.

I love hearing these. Please email with the situation, the choice, and what you want to happen.
